Scratch Game

This scratch game is about a spaceship trying to defense earth from space invaders. There are 2 types of space invaders: the minions and the bosses. A minion has 3 lives, and a boss has 20 lives. Instructions are shown in the game. We could have included a shop to improve weapons or to purchase new equipments.


To play this game, you have to control the dragon in the dark dungeon to catch all the golden coins and try to avoid the grey spiky balls. Each time you catch a coin you will gain 1 point for the score, and you will lose 1 live out of 3 whenever you catch a grey spiky ball. Be a hero!
